who is this guy?

strangeness is neither earned nor learned, it's simply gifted by luck.

I have been very lucky throughout my life, and throughout this strange journey called life, I have accrued many skills, passions, and curiosities in the fields of music, engineering, and every combination thereof.

professionally, I offer a unique perspectives in problem solving rooted in first principles. when solving creative problems I often try to draw parallels to problems in math and engineering, and vice versa.

while the idea that knowledge of calculus or group theory has anything to do with writing a beautiful orchestral piece seems absurd, I truly believe the same rigorous processes that underscore the technical world also describe the creative world.
